About Buddies

We're a collection of 4,200 smoking frens who come in a variety of shapes and sizes. There's a Buddy for everyone, regardless of how you prefer to smoke.

Buddies is truly a labor of love and passion project by the team. We want to share our enthusiasm for the marijuana/stoner culture with the world through this project and allow others to do the same. There is so much more ahead for Buddies past the NFTs themselves. We can hardly wait for you all to see what we have in store. Your Buddies can’t wait to meet you!
